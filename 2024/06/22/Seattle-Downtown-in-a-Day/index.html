🚗 2024 May: Seattle Downtown in a Day

Gloria Su
2024-06-22
Journey
Journey, Seattle

1. Space Needle ⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️

Official Website https://www.spaceneedle.com/
Directions 400 Broad St, Seattle, WA 98109
Operating Hours 8:00AM – 12:00AM (may change)
Highlights
  • Two-Level Observation Deck: The upper level offers open-air views with glass walls and benches, while the lower level features a rotating glass floor and a restaurant perfect for sunset views.
    		•
    Personal Preference I think it's definitely worth a visit. The CityPass includes both day and night tickets, and I recommend checking the sunset time and going in the evening for the night ticket.

    2. Museum of Pop Culture ⭐️⭐️⭐️

    Official Website https://www.mopop.org/
    Directions 325 5th Avenue N, Seattle, WA 98109
    Operating Hours 10:00 AM – 5:00 PM
    Highlights
  • First and Second Floors: Explore exhibitions dedicated to music, sci-fi, and gaming.
  • Second Floor: Check out the horror-themed area (not for the faint-hearted) and the instrument experience zone.
  • Third Floor: Home to special and temporary exhibits.
    		•
    Personal Preference A must-visit for those who love pop culture and history. They have exhibits showcasing famous movie costumes and props.

    3. Chihuly Garden and Glass ⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️

    Official Website https://www.chihulygardenandglass.com/
    Directions 305 Harrison St, Seattle, WA 98109
    Operating Hours 8:30 AM — 5:30PM, Glasshouse closed: 4:00 pm
    Highlights
  • Glass Artwork by Dale Chihuly: Featuring eight galleries, an outdoor garden, and a gift shop.
    		•
    Personal Preference Every art installation I saw was absolutely stunning.

    4. Seattle Aquarium ⭐️⭐️

    Official Website https://www.seattleaquarium.org/
    Directions 1483 Alaskan Way, Pier 59, Seattle, WA 98101
    Operating Hours 9:30 AM⁠ –⁠ 6 PM, LAST ENTRY AT 5 PM
    Highlights
  • Underwater Dome: Get an immersive experience with marine life.
  • Otters, Octopuses, and Seabirds: Watch these creatures up close.
  • Touch Pool: Interactive experience for all ages.
    		•
    Personal Preference It's a very small aquarium, so I wouldn't recommend going out of your way to visit.

    5. Argosy Cruises Harbor Tour ⭐️⭐️⭐️

    Official Website https://www.argosycruises.com/argosy-cruises/harbor-cruise/
    Directions Pier 55 on the Seattle Waterfront – 1101 Alaskan Way, Seattle, WA 98101
    Operating Hours Check at the official website.
    Highlights
  • 1-hour narrated cruise: See all of the beauty and history of the Emerald City.
    		•
    Personal Preference The one-hour cruise comes with a guided narration throughout. I suggest sitting on the top open deck on the right side for the best views.

    6. The Seattle Great Wheel ⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️

    Official Website https://seattlegreatwheel.com/
    Directions Pier 57 - Miners Landing, 1301 Alaskan Way, Seattle, WA 98101
    Operating Hours 10:00 AM - 10:00 PM (may change)
    Highlights
  • Sunset Views: Enjoy 3-5 rounds on the Ferris wheel with stunning sunset views over the bay.
    		•
    Personal Preference The lighting is perfect for photos, and you can enjoy the sunset from the Ferris wheel.

    7. Pike Place Market ⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️

    Official Website https://www.pikeplacemarket.org/
    Directions 85 Pike Street, Seattle, WA 98101
    Operating Hours 10:00 AM - 6:00 PM (different by store)
    Highlights
  • Original Starbucks: Visit the first Starbucks store with its classic two-tailed mermaid logo – perfect for a souvenir mug.
  • Piroshky Piroshky: Try the famous Beef and Onion/Cheese pastries from this Russian bakery.
  • Pike Place Chowder: Indulge in clam chowder starting at $7.75, or treat yourself to a $35 lobster roll.
  • Market Attractions: Don't miss the quirky Gum Wall, the golden pig statue, and the market sign.
  • MarketFront Overlook: Enjoy views of the Great Wheel and the bay from this scenic viewpoint.
    		•
    Personal Preference This is the best place to visit! It's lively and great for strolling around. I tasted lots of delicious food and would gladly go again.
